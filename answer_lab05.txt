Question: What are the differences between the Swing and AWT implementation?
Feature			AWT					Swing
Component Type		Heavyweight (native)			Lightweight (Java-based)
Look and Feel		Platform-dependent			Pluggable, customizable
Components		Basic GUI components			Advanced GUI components
Performance		Faster for simple GUIs			Slightly slower but flexible
Customization		Limited	Highly customizable
Thread Safety		Minimal	Requires EDT for updates
Modern Use		Legacy, lightweight GUIs		Rich, modern GUIs
